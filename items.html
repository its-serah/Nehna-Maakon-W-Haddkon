14. Help Us Support Families in Need

Many families have been forced to flee their homes, seeking refuge in schools and temporary shelters. These individuals, including children and the elderly, urgently need basic necessities such as water, food, mattresses, and hygiene kits.

Your generous donations will provide immediate relief to those in need. Every contribution, no matter how small, will make a significant difference.

For Donations, DM us on Instagram: @feedtheneed.lb
